Early Life

Albert Einstein was born in March 14, 1897 in Ulm, Germany. Later on, when he was 16, he moved to Switzerland. He received his college degree in Swiss Fedral Polytechnic and his ph.D in University of Zurich. By the way Einstein didn't fail his math class in elementary school.

Innovations

The most famou discovery by Einstein is the formula E=mc^2 for energy and mass. Other theories developed by Einstein includes general relativity, Bose-Einstein theory, also the development of atomic bomb.

Awards

Einstein has won the 1921 and 1922 Nobel Prize in Physics.
